(phone rings)

An operator sees details about you.
Your mom added your phone number and personal details on a website.
The operator now knows you want to talk with simple words and clear steps.

Screen shows active video call.
An operator answers the call.
You see your face in the top left.
The operator cannot be seen.
You can flip the camera, end the call, and open the text chat.
The operator starts asking questions.

You say: "Yes."
The operator asks: "Do you need police, fire, or medical?"
You say: "Medical."
The operator asks: "Where are you?"

The operator asks: "Which city is your mom's house at?"
You say: "Burnaby."
The operator asks: "What street is your mom's house on?"
You say: "Henning Drive."
The operator asks: "Do you know the house number?"
You say: "3999, I think."

floor you are on?"
You say: "Four."

An operator sees your location on a map.
From the location you said, the operator now knows where you are.

Screen shows video call again.
