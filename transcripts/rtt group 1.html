Using real time text in an emergency.

(man snoring)
Man sleeping in bed.
On the right is a table with a cell phone.
You are sleeping when you hear a window break.

View outside room.
You see light downstairs and hear voices.

Man in closet with the door closed.
You lock the door, grab your cell phone and hide in the closet.

Cell phone appears.
You open the phone app.
Phone app opens. There is a phone keypad.
You press 911 then you tap the call button.
You call fast so people do not hear.
(phone buttons beeping)
You see three choices: Call, RTT/TTY Call, and RTT/TTY Relay Call.
You choose RTT/TTY Call.
The call is being made.
(phone ringing)

Cell phone with a 911 call.
An operator answers the call.
The RTT chat button is enabled.
You tap the RTT button.

Text chat window appears.
Text typed is sent right away. There is no send button.
You can turn on the mic to speak.
The operator types: "911. Do you need police, fire, or medical?"
You reply: "I do not get it. Use easy words."
The operator types: "Do you need help?"
You reply: "Yes."
The operator types: "Is there a thief?"
You reply: "Yes."
The operator types: "Where are you?"
You reply: "I am at my mom's house."
The operator types: "Which city is your mom's house at?"
You reply: "Burnaby."
The operator types: "What street is your mom's house on?"
You reply: "Henning Drive."
The operator types: "Do you know the house number?"
You reply: "3999 I think."
You see the next question before you are done.
The operator types: "Which floor?"
You reply: "Two."
The operator types: "Is your phone number 604-473-9363?"
You reply: "I think so."
The operator types: "What is your first and last name?"
You reply: "Nathan Frost."

Your call is handed off to someone else.
The operator types: "Burnaby police. Where is the thief?"
You reply: "The thief is downstairs."
The operator types: "Did you lock the door?"
You reply: "Yes."
The operator types: "Is there anyone else at home?"
You reply: "No."
The operator types: "Are you hiding?"
You reply: "Yes. In a closet."
The operator types: "What noises did you hear?"
You reply: "I heard glass break. There are voices."
The operator types: "More than one voice?"
You reply: "Yes."
The operator types: "Male or female voices?"
You reply: "Female."
The operator types: "Okay. Help is coming."
The operator types: I will stay on. Tell me if something happens.

Image of flashing lights.
(police siren)
